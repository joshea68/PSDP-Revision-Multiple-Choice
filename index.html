Sources & further reading — download the regulations and guidance

All sources below are free to download. The HSA and Irish Statute Book are the authoritative starting points; Engineers Ireland and ACEI host the joint industry guidance referenced throughout the course.

1. Official legal text — Irish Statute Book

  • S.I. No. 291/2013 — Safety, Health and Welfare at Work (Construction) Regulations 2013 (HSA PDF, verified) (the core regulations — direct PDF download from the HSA)
  • S.I. No. 291/2013 — same regulations on the Irish Statute Book (verified) (canonical legal text)
  • Act No. 10 of 2005 — Safety, Health and Welfare at Work Act 2005 (framework legislation; see ss.15, 17, 58)
  • S.I. No. 299/2007 — Safety, Health and Welfare at Work (General Application) Regulations 2007
  • S.I. No. 386/2006 — Exposure to Asbestos Regulations 2006
  • S.I. No. 589/2010 — Exposure to Asbestos (Amendment) Regulations 2010
  • irishstatutebook.ie — for any other S.I.s or amending instruments

2. HSA — guidance and forms (all links go to a direct PDF unless noted)

  • Guidelines on the Procurement, Design and Management Requirements of the SHWW (Construction) Regulations 2013 (verified) (the main companion document)
  • Summary of Key Duties under the Procurement, Design and Site Management Requirements (verified)
  • Clients in Construction — Best Practice Guidance (verified)
  • Getting Construction Work Done Safely — Guide for Homeowners (verified)
  • HSA Asbestos topic page (landing page — the ACM Practical Guidelines PDF is linked from here)
  • Form AF1 — Notification to HSA before the design process begins (verified)
  • HSA Publications → Construction (landing page) (for everything else, including the Guide for Contractors and Project Supervisors)

3. HSA Codes of Practice (all direct PDFs, verified)

  • Code of Practice for Safety in Roofwork (2016)
  • Code of Practice for Access and Working Scaffolds (2023) (2023 edition; supersedes the 2008 COP referenced in the slides)
  • Code of Practice for Working in Confined Spaces (2017)
  • Code of Practice for Avoiding Danger from Underground Services (2016)
  • Code of Practice for Avoiding Danger from Overhead Electricity Lines (May 2019) (May 2019 edition; supersedes the 2008 COP referenced in the slides)
  • Code of Practice for the Design and Installation of Anchors (2017)
  • Code of Practice for Contractors with Three or Less Employees Working on Roads (2016)

4. Engineers Ireland / ACEI / RIAI — joint industry guidance (landing pages — most of these are paid CPD publications or members-only documents, not free PDFs)

  • ACEI Publications (catalogue / order form) — Briefing Notes 1, 2 and 3 and the Advice Notes on PW/TW Certificates and Anchors are ordered from here
  • Engineers Ireland — Designing for Safety in Construction (CPD course) (the joint EI / ACEI / RIAI manual by Jack Kavanagh is delivered through this course)
  • Engineers Ireland — Project Supervisor Design Process (PSDP) course
  • HSA — Project Supervisor Design Process (PSDP) topic page (useful summary of the PSDP role and links to all relevant HSA documents)

5. Reporting and notification

  • AF1 notifications by registered post: HSA, The Metropolitan Building, James Joyce Street, Dublin 1
  • AF1 by e-mail (HSA-directed): wcu@hsa.ie
